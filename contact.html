FAQ

Common Inquiries

Everything you need to know about Loru — from sizing to collaborations and beyond.

01

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

Our team typically responds to all inquiries within 1–2 business days. For urgent matters, we recommend reaching out via our contact form with a clear subject line, and we'll prioritize accordingly. We're committed to providing timely, thoughtful replies to every message we receive.

02

Does Loru offer partnership or wholesale opportunities?

Yes — we welcome conversations with retailers, boutiques, and select distribution partners who share our vision for modern luxury fashion. If you're interested in a wholesale or partnership arrangement, please reach out through our contact page with details about your business and we'll be in touch.

03

How do I get in touch with the Loru media or press team?

For editorial features, press requests, lookbook access, or campaign imagery, please contact our media team directly through the contact form, selecting "Press & Media" as your inquiry type. We aim to support journalists, stylists, and publications with high-resolution assets and brand materials promptly.

04

Where can I find sizing guidance for women's and men's apparel?

Detailed size charts for both women apparel and menswear are available on each individual product page. Our guides include measurements in both centimetres and inches. If you're between sizes or need personalised advice, our support team is happy to help you find the right fit — simply send us your measurements and the item you're considering.

05

Is Loru open to brand collaborations and creative projects?

Absolutely. Loru is passionate about collaborating with artists, designers, photographers, and like-minded luxury streetwear brands that align with our aesthetic ethos. Whether it's a capsule collection, a co-branded campaign, or a creative installation, we're open to exploring meaningful partnerships. Send us your proposal and portfolio to start the conversation.

06

How can I get general support for my order or account?

For all general support — including order tracking, returns, exchanges, or account queries — please use our contact form or email us directly. Our customer care team operates Monday through Friday and will ensure your experience with Loru is seamless from the moment you browse to the moment your order arrives.
